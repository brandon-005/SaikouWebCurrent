Community Rules

We built our games for players to have fun. Creating a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone is a crucial part of what we're about, and these rules are what we ask and expect players to abide by to achieve this.

The Roblox Terms of Service contain rules that apply to your use of Saikou's games, so some of these rules may look familiar. However, Saikou offers so many ways to interact with others through its games that it benefits from an additional level of explanation as a helpful resource.

Our Community Rules apply to ALL players of Saikou's games. These standards are a guide on how you should interact within our incredible, globe-spanning community. Just because something isn't explicitly called out below doesn't mean it's OK. We all bring something unique, and that uniqueness is worth protecting.

As these rules apply to everyone, it's also vital that you do not encourage, help, or support others in breaking these rules. It's never fun to lose access to games, but know that we do so to help ensure a positive gaming experience for our community when we put restrictions in place.

Personal Information

The Saikou community runs on trust, and we must protect the privacy of our players. We take this responsibility seriously, and we won't tolerate behaviour or content that puts the privacy or security of another player at risk. Following this, you're never allowed to share other people's personal information. Threatening to share someone's real-world location, real name, etc. is also not allowed.

Intolerance and Discrimination

Hate and discrimination come in many forms, but none have a home here. To make the Saikou community a place where everyone can hang out and feel welcomed, we all need to play our part. This means keeping in mind how players could interpret your messages and leaving those unsavoury jokes outside the community. The Saikou community welcomes diversity in race, ethnicity, colour, religion, gender identity, and other groups. We won't stand for demeaning, marginalising or belittling content and will immediately take the necessary action.

Cheating

Saikou builds its games to be played moderately and within the rules. Cheating, griefing, or exploiting bugs and glitches is not allowed, along with promoting or advertising known cheats, bugs, or exploits. Exploiting known glitches or cheating doesn't just affect Saikou. These actions also inhibit the ability of Saikou to provide the best possible gaming experience to players around the world. If you encounter someone cheating or using bugs as a means of gaining an unfair advantage, report it on our Discord server.
